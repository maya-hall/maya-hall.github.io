About Me

Hi, and welcome to my portfolio!

I am an enthusiastic and dedicated GIS, remote sensing, and research professional with a background in ecology and environmental science. My work experience includes data analysis, cartography, field studies, and program coordination. With over 8 years of professional and scientific expertise, I am excited to contribute to new projects, community-motivated programs, and teams that aim to protect, conserve, and restore our vital natural spaces. Thanks for stopping by!
